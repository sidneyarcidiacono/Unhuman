About Unhuman

Unhuman is subversion.

Unhuman is a project centered around the deconstruction of Self, and of identity. It demands discomfort, requiring that the viewer sit in that uncanny psychological place between human and not-quite. Inspired by the necessary deconstruction of an identity that was dangerous to the artist's survival, Unhuman explores the grief, discomfort, and difficulty in letting go, and burying a Self that no longer fits. Examining processes of moving forward, sacred change and finding peace in rebirth, it uses symbols from dreams, omens presented to the artist during the period of their regeneration.

About the Artist

Sid is a self-taught painter, sculptor and illustrator currently based in Oakland, California. Unhuman is the culmination of themes that have been omnipresent throughout their life, a reflection of their dreams and experiences as they relate to the darker side of human psychology and sensation.
